Members

Wish to join?
2600 Australia is a loose-knit group of people interested in computer security, electronic gadgetry, communications and technology in general. As such, there is no official membership conditions or requirements. All you need to do is show up at a meeting or get involved online.

However, if you feel you have something extra to give to the group, consider submitting a volunteer application form, describing how you feel you can assist the 2600 Australia chapter.
